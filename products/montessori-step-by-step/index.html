Montessori Step-by-Step

weekly inspiration that follows your child

So you’ve decided you want to put Montessori principles into practice in your home. While it’s easy to get started, truly changing your parenting habits takes a commitment to consistent effort over the long-haul.

Montessori Step-by-Step aims to be your partner in this ongoing journey. Every week, based on your child’s age, you’ll receive an email with reminders and resources to help you do Montessori.
